Football

Football what is it?

Football is one of the most popular sports around the world. It consists of two teams scoring against each other in a given time frame. There are two halfs which both are 45 minutes each. The aim of both teams is to either defend or attack the opposite teams goals. There are 11 players in both teams and there can be a few subsitiutes in case some of them get injured while playing. This is very useful as some players can be subbed off and a fresh player can play one half and another can play the second.

Skills in football such as dribbling and passing the ball are key as that is one of the main ways of progressing through the game. This is the way a formation can be used to progress the game forward. These can be developed using drills such as using cones to run inbetween or use cones to pass the balls to in case there isnt a person available to assist you with it.

Hockey

Hockey what is it?

Hockey is a brilliant sport that consists of two teams that score against eachother using a hockey stick and a ball. The rules of the game are simple you have two teams which both cannot handle the ball with their bodies or hands. They are only allowed to move their sticks using this they are able to control the ball. Both teams are opposite eachother and can set formations of the way they want to play. There are 11 players in each team it can consist of 4 mid-feilders 4 3 attackers 3 defenders and a single goalkeeper. However this is based on a very common formation teams are able to adjust to how they wish to play in order to fulfill strategies such as doing something different to throw the oppositions concentration.

This is a great sport to engage a players body. It works on 3 main skills. The 3 skills are stamina, hand eye coordination and speed. Stamina is very important in maintaining a succesful play or strategy. Hockey requires players to run around for a very long time and so it is very crucial to keep stamina and this can be done through cardio training and long distance running. This will help build stamina and increase the amount of energy you can save which is essential in being a good hockey player. Hand eye coordination is the second most important skill. This is also crucial because the ball is small and the field is full of other players. The presence of mind is a players most important role as they should be aware of what is going on in the field so that they can adjust their strategies. The final skill to work on is speed. This can be developed in the cardio process, it requires healthy diet and regular running to become good at.

Running

Running what is it?

Running is very important and can be applied almost to all sports. This is cardio and can be improved and developed on simply by doing it regularly. The more you run the better it is for your body and as you get used to it you are able to run longer distances and at quicker paces. This works out your legs and strengthens the bones which is helpful in every day tasks. It keeps your body engaged and is a great way to stay in shape. This sport is very popular in the olympics. Usain bolt has popularisedthis sport by being the fastest recorded man in history in the 100m sprint and the relay.

There are some things that can be worked on to improve our bodies and run more efficeintly. Things such as having a schedule of running or commuting to places by running is important in developing running skills. This overtime will allow you to get used to running for longer periods and would be a day to day thing and a means of travel for a person.

Cricket

Cricket what is it?

Cricket is a very interesting and hard sport. There are many different formats which you are able to play cricket but the most loved is test cricket. This is the oldest format where both teams dress up in white clothes and face eachother in an unlimited over game. This means if a team is put into bat they will have the chance to score as many runs as they can and after they are satisfied with the amount of runs they have made or been bowled out they can declare innings and let the other team chase down the amount of runs. Cricket requires agility speed and quick arm action.

Agility is gained through regular training and a scheduled day. A cricketer should stick to a strict routine when they are in camp for training. This is essential in being a successful player because these steps help develop skills. Something as small as healthy diet can improve agility eating the correct foods can help give that extra boost in a players confidence. Speed is important for both the batsmen and bowlers. This is because they may need to chase the ball or run inbetween the wickets. A quick arm action is important again for everyone because a bowler can speed the ball up to put off the batsmen or the batsmen can use this speed to hit the ball harder. This can be developed through practicing drills such as the certain arm actions used to do certain types of actions.
